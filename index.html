The Gang de la Sinfonia (GDLS or SYGG, previously known as Symphony Gang) is a multi-generational organization dedicated to cybersecurity and hacking. With members spanning different age groups, we actively participate in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions and various hacking activities. Currently ranked 6th nationwide in Canada, our diverse expertise, ranging from seasoned professionals to enthusiastic newcomers, fosters a collaborative environment where knowledge is shared and skills are honed.
